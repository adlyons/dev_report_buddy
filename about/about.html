Report_Buddy

A simple web app to assist severe weather reporting for Twitter/X.

Co-authored by: Andrew Lyons (NWS SPC) and Hunter Hurley.

Simply host Report_Buddy.html on an HTTPS-enabled web server to use. This is an open-source project designed to help increase public awareness of severe weather. Feel free to fork and modify it for your own use.

We maintain no liability for the use of this software. Use at your own risk.

For best compatibility, use a modern third-party browser such as Chrome or Firefox for now.
